What do these colors mean?

Yellow product & countdown:

Yellow productmeans the product has maximum cPs/cost. It's best deal (Does not consider buying upgrades yet).

A countdownshows after how long you can buy the yellow productif your cookies is not enough for now.

Green product:

Green productis not the best one, but buying it can help you buy yellow product faster.
